Speedometer App

A clean, GPS-based Android application to accurately track your pace. Whether it's a casual ride, this app records everything.

Changelog
  • V2 features: Bilingual support, Odometer (current session and total distance), safety confirmation dialogs when stopping or resetting, dynamic app name, optimized navigation bar.
  • Real-time speed tracking (km/h and mph)
  • Maximum and average speed statistics
  • Current session and total distance (Odometer)
  • Minimalist, light-dark design
  • No unnecessary ads, just pure data
